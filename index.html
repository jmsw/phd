Some Common Questions About my PhD Project

What is my project about?

My project is about Isabella Beeton, who wrote a very famous cookbook in London in 1861 called the Book of Household Management. She died very young, at age 28. Although a lot of people think of her as a stereotypical Victorian domestic expert, based on the reputation of her cookbook, that is not the whole story. She was also a writer and editor for The Englishwoman's Domestic Magazine, which was the first secular women's magazine in Britain. By tracking down previously hard-to-find copies of her magazine work, and looking closely at what she wrote, my project tells a more complete story of her remarkable working life.
